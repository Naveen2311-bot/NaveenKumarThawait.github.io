About

Working at the seam between intelligent systems and the networks they run on.

Naveen is a PhD Research Scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur — a central university in Chhattisgarh, India. His work develops and evaluates machine learning frameworks and deep learning algorithms for predictive modelling and applied AI, with a research throughline that runs from IoT security and sustainable wireless networking to cybercrime detection and prevention. Earlier work spans generative modelling, applied diagnostics, and materials-science prediction — reflecting a consistent interest in where learning algorithms meet real-world constraints.
